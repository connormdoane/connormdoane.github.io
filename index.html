Experience

Published Astrophysical Journal Research Paper

I helped author a research paper on solar flares, which disproved the leading scientific theory on coronal heating. By analyzing various solar flares, it was found that the emissions can't account for the amount of thermal energy measured in the corona of the sun, which was previously believed to be the cause of the temperature disparity. This research was published in the Astrophysical Journal in 2023.
